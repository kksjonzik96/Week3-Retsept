Strawberry Milkshake

Ingredient Amount
Milk 500 ml
Frozen banana 1
Almond milk 1 cup
Pure maple syrup 1 tablespoon
Protein powder 1 scoop
  1. Step: Place ingredients into the blender cup and blend until combined
  2. Step: Pour into individual glasses and top with optional toppings.

Notes

If you have a larger blender, you may consider doubling this recipe for easier blending. Depending on the size or cut of your strawberries, you may need to add a little extra milk. Sometimes I have to add an extra ¼ – ½ cup. Also, if your strawberries are super sweet you may not need any extra sweetener. Add a scoop of dairy free vanilla ice cream or use canned coconut milk for extra creaminess!
